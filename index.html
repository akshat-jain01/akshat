What role does graphic design play in my website?
Creates Attraction

Graphic design is an essential aspect of web development as it contributes to the visual appeal, functionality, and user experience of websites.
It encompasses creating visual elements such as layouts, typography, color schemes, and graphics to enhance the overall quality and effectiveness of websites.

Key elements of graphic design that should be incorporated into a website include color schemes, typography, images, icons, and overall layout to create a cohesive and visually appealing design.
